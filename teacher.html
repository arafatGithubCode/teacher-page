Set Work Instantly

Step 02

Seamless Submissions

Students can submit work via direct file upload, Google Drive integration, or simple text entry. View all pending submissions in a unified dashboard organized by class.

AI-Assisted Feedback

Sometimes text isn't enough. Record quick, personal voice messages to explain complex concepts or offer encouragement. It adds a human touch to digital grading.

Instant Returns

With one click, return graded work to the entire class or specific students. Students receive notifications instantly and can view their feedback on any device.
